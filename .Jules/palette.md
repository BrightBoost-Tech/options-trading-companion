## 2025-02-12 - Compose Trade Form Accessibility
**Learning:** Native `label` elements in React must use `htmlFor` combined with matching `id`s on their inputs. Relying solely on visual proximity is insufficient for screen readers and breaks the native ability to click the label text to focus the input field, which is especially important for dropdowns (`select`) and date pickers.
**Action:** When creating new forms or adding inputs, always generate unique `id`s and explicitly link them to `label` tags via `htmlFor`. Also, ensure async submit buttons always have a `disabled` state with a loading spinner to prevent double-submissions and provide immediate visual feedback.
