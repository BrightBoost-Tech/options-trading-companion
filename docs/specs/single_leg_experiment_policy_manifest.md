- **axis A** (`single_leg_optin_block`): opt-in block **present** (experimental) vs **absent** (control)
- **axis B** (`base_family`): **aggressive** (throughput arm) vs **conservative** (conviction arm)

Each experimental differs from its matched control on **axis A only** (the base 11 PolicyConfig fields are byte-identical within a pair); the two experimentals differ on **axis B only** (identical opt-in block). So a single-leg result is attributable to the flag (matched-pair contrast) or to throughput/conviction (arm contrast), never confounded.

## Epoch & non-collision

All four rows live in `effective_epoch = single_leg_experiment_v1` — DISTINCT from the fleet epoch `small_tier_v1`.
